Who is Priyanka?

Priyanka Rana, a 22-year-old professional whitewater kayaker from Uttarakhand, has kayaking in her blood. Growing up beside the Ganga River, she was naturally drawn to the sport, inspired by her family’s kayaking legacy. With years of practice and dedication, Priyanka has developed an exceptional mastery over whitewater kayaking, making her one of India’s finest kayakers. She has competed in multiple national and international kayaking events, winning accolades for her skill, agility, and courage on the water. Through Kayak Ganga, Priyanka aims to introduce others to the thrill and beauty of whitewater kayaking. Her vision is to make kayaking accessible to everyone—from beginners to seasoned adventurers—while promoting Uttarakhand as a premier destination for adventure sports.
