About Us

Welcome to Kayak Ganga, your gateway to experiencing the thrill and adventure of whitewater kayaking in Rishikesh. Founded to promote the sport of kayaking and share the beauty of the Ganga River, Kayak Ganga offers world-class kayaking courses, guided expeditions, and adventure packages for all skill levels. Whether you’re a first-timer looking to get your feet wet or an experienced paddler seeking to challenge yourself, we’ve got something for everyone.

With our team of certified kayaking instructors, led by none other than Priyanka Rana, a professional whitewater kayaker from Uttarakhand, Kayak Ganga provides the expertise and guidance needed to master the rapids safely. Priyanka, with her deep-rooted connection to the Ganga River and multiple national and international accolades, plays a pivotal role in making Kayak Ganga a trusted name in the kayaking community. Our mission is to make kayaking not just a sport, but a lifelong passion for adventurers. From training beginners to navigating challenging rapids, Kayak Ganga strives to create a safe, enjoyable, and memorable kayaking experience for all.
